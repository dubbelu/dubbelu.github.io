Healthspan

Longevity for everyone

The unfortunate truth about aging is that most people either loses the abilty to be active and fully enjoy life - or they just don't get that old. It doesn't have to be that way.

In the collective human knowlegebase are many techniques, routines, diets and medical interventions that can allow the average person to increase their odds of reaching old age, and to do so in good health.

You shouldn't have to be a millionaire or longevity scientist to reap these benefits. Healthspan adapts to your circumstances to create a tailored made plan, focused on the highest impact actions for you, to get you there.

Open Infra

Public funds, public code

Our society runs on code. All public functions - schools, hopsitals, unemployment agenices and other government agencies - rely on tailor made software to perform their functions.

This software is enormously expensive to create and is unfortunately kept private. Other agencies or private individuals and companies cannot use the code that we all paid for through our taxes!

Open Infra is an open source initiative to replace properietary code with open source code in the public sphere.

TikToken

Liquidity for digital cashflow

Content creators are the new entreprenours. Their reach is unmatched by traditional channels and their ability to monetize into cashflow is excellent.

But what do you do as a content creator if you need money upfront to finance a project - or a house purchase?

With Tiktoken, you can issue tokens, that gives the holder rights to a percentage of your cashflow for a set period of time, and sell to their fanbase. Creators get cash upfront and your most diehard fans get upside on your continued growth.
